FAQs- DAI PARK PREMIER Q&A Collection

Hotel FAQs

  1. Q(1): What are the check-in and check-out times?
    A: Check-in: From 14:00, Check-out: Until 12:00.
  2. Q(2): Could you please provide information about late check-out?
    A: Late check-out is available for a fee as follows:
    - Until 18:00: Half the price of one night
    - After 18:00: Price of one night
    * Depending on availability, we may not be able to accommodate late check-outs, so please contact us in advance if needed.
    * Additionally, you can relax at our attached "Dia Lounge" (free until 23:00) until your departure.
  3. Q(3): Please explain how to use the laundry service.
    A: Please place your clothes in the provided laundry bag along with the laundry list. We will collect them by 10:00 in the morning and deliver them back to your room around 19:00 the same day.
    * You can use up to 10 items per day with the DIA BUSINESS PACK (500Rs+TAX).
  4. Q(4): What is the policy for early check-in?
    A: The early check-in fee is as follows:
    - Before 7:00 am: Price of one night
    - After 7:00 am: Half the price of one night (* Breakfast is available at an additional cost.)
  5. Q(5): Is it possible to send luggage to the hotel in advance?
    A: Yes, it's possible. We will need to confirm your name, contents of the shipment, and expected arrival date in advance.
    * Please inquire separately for large items or other specific requests.
  6. Q(6): What should I do if I lock my room key inside the room?
    A: Please contact the front desk. We will provide you with a replacement key. Once you unlock your room, please return the original key to the front desk.
  7. Q(7): When does the cancellation fee apply?
    A: The change/cancellation fees are as follows:
    - From 48 hours before check-in time (14:00): 100% of the first night's stay
    - No-show (cancellation without notice): 100% of the first night's stay
    * For changes or cancellations on the day of arrival, please contact the hotel.
    * Example: Cancellations for stays scheduled on June 10th are free until 14:00 on June 8th.
  8. Q(8): Is it possible for two people to stay in one room?
    A: Yes, it's possible. Please inform us when making a reservation.
    * An additional charge of +2,000Rs+TAX per person will apply.
  9. Q(9): Can children stay with us?
    A: Yes, children under 12 can stay for free.
    * For children aged 12 and above, an additional charge of +2,000Rs+TAX per person will apply as adult fare.
  10. Q(10): Is it possible to add an extra bed?
    A: Yes, it's possible. It's free to add an extra bed only in Executive rooms.
    * Please inquire as availability may vary depending on the season.
  11. Q(11): I'd like to send my luggage from the hotel.
    A: Yes, it's possible. Please contact the front desk for details on fees, which may vary based on weight and distance.
  12. Q(12): Is it possible to leave luggage after check-out?
    A: Yes, it's possible. Please contact the front desk.
  13. Q(13): What amenities are provided in the room?
    A: Various amenities are provided including:
    - Room amenities: Air purifier, Washlet*, Internet (Wi-Fi), Work desk, Safe, Mineral water, Tea, Coffee, Telephone (international calls available), Wall-mounted TV, Minibar, Hairdryer
    - Bathroom amenities: Hand shower, Bath towel, Hand towel, Face towel, Bath mat, Bathrobe, Comb, Shampoo, Moisturizer, Shaving set, Toothbrush set, Shower cap
    * Washlet is a registered trademark of TOTO.

Dining FAQs

  1. Q(1): What time does breakfast start?
    A: Monday to Saturday: 6:30 - 10:00 (Last order at 9:30)
    Sunday: 7:00 - 10:30 (Last order at 10:00)
    * Breakfast is served at the Cafe & Restaurant "Honey" on the 1st floor.
  2. Q(2): I only want to use the breakfast at the hotel.
    A: Non-hotel guests can avail breakfast for 900Rs+TAX per person.
    * Please inform the restaurant staff when using the service.
  3. Q(3): What are the restaurant's operating hours?
    A: Cafe & Restaurant "Honey" on the 1st floor:
    - 12:00 - 15:30 (Last order at 15:00)
    - 18:00 - 23:00 (Last order at 22:30)
    Japanese restaurants "Raifu" and "Raifu-tei" on the 2nd floor:
    - 12:00 - 14:30 (Last order at 14:00)
    - 18:00 - 23:00 (Last order at 22:30)
  4. Q(4): Is there room service?
    A: Room service is available from 22:30 to 7:00 the next morning.
    Please dial extension 2044 to place your order.
    * Payment can be made in cash, credit, or room charge.
    * Limited to light snack menus.
  5. Q(5): Can I pre-order bread?
    A: Please inquire as the availability of bread may vary depending on the season.
    * Please understand that we cannot guarantee availability due to machine trouble or material shortage.
  6. Q(6): Can I purchase beverages within the hotel?
    A: You can purchase beverages at the reception of the 2nd basement floor, near the bath/gym area.
    * Minibar operating hours: Monday to Friday 15:00 - 24:00, Saturday/Sunday 11:00 - 24:00
  7. Q(7): Are there private dining rooms available?
    A: There is a private dining room "Raifu-tei" on the 2nd floor.
    * Please call here for reservations: TEL +91-124-4566701 (English support)

Facilities and Miscellaneous FAQs

  1. Q(1): What are the operating hours for the bath (2nd basement)?
    A: [Male]
    Monday to Friday: 3:00 PM - 12:00 AM
    Saturday/Sunday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 AM
    [Female]
    Monday to Friday: 3:00 PM - 10:30 PM
    Saturday/Sunday: 9:00 AM - 10:30 PM
    - Last check-in for hotel guests: [Male] 11:30 PM, [Female] 10:00 PM
    - Last check-in for external users: [Male/Female] 10:00 PM
    * External user fee: Adults 500Rs (incl. tax) per person, Under 20s 250Rs (incl. tax) per person, Under 6s/free
    * Towels, shampoo, conditioner, body soap, and hairdryer are provided.
  2. Q(2): How can I exchange foreign currency?
    A: Please provide your room number and the amount at the front desk.
    * Exchange rates may vary daily, please check the board next to the front desk.
    * We cannot provide reverse currency exchange (e.g., Rupees to Japanese Yen) and post-checkout exchanges.
  3. Q(3): How do I connect to Wi-Fi?
    A: We will provide you with a Wi-Fi code during check-in. Select "Diapark" and enter the code.
    * You can connect up to two devices with one code.
  4. Q(4): I want to reserve a banquet hall.
    A: For banquet hall reservations, please contact the following:
    TEL: +91-9999477553
    E-MAIL: srm@parkpremierhotels.com / sales@diapark.jp
  5. Q(5): What are the operating hours for the souvenir shop "Frangipani"?
    A: Operating hours are as follows:
    Monday to Sunday: 10:00 AM - 10:30 PM
    Contact Information:
    TEL: +91-9958873151
    E-MAIL: mail@frangipani-india.com
  6. Q(6): What type of electrical outlets are used in India?
    A: The main types of electrical outlets in India are B3, BF, B, and C types. Our hotel rooms use B3 type outlets.
  7. Q(7): Is it possible to borrow plug adapters?
    A: Yes, it is possible. Please inquire at the front desk.
    * Please note that there is a limited number of adapters available, so we may not be able to lend them out in some cases.
  8. Q(8): The TV won't turn on.
    A: It may take some time to start up.
    * If the issue persists after some time, please contact the front desk.
  9. Q(9): Which credit cards are accepted?
    A: VISA, MasterCard, AMEX, and JCB are accepted.
  10. Q(10): Is there an ATM in the hotel?
    A: There is no ATM in the hotel. HDFCBANK is within a 5-minute walk.
  11. Q(11): Are there any shops nearby for shopping?
    A: Within a 20-minute drive from the hotel, you can find Ambience Mall (large mall), South Point Mall (mall/groceries/daily necessities), and Spencer's (groceries/daily necessities).
  12. Q(12): Can I watch Japanese TV programs?
    A: You can enjoy programs from "NHK World/NHK World Premium" in your room.
  13. Q(13): Is there a smoking area within the hotel?
    A: There is no designated smoking area inside the building. Please use the smoking area next to the hotel entrance.
  14. Q(14): Can I purchase tobacco within the hotel?
    A: Tobacco is available for purchase at Cafe Restaurant "Honey" on the 1st floor.
  15. Q(15): Can I make external or international calls from the room phone?
    A: Yes, it is possible (charges apply).
    - Dial "0" before the phone number: for calls within Gurugram
    - Dial "00" before the phone number: for calls within India but outside Gurugram
    - Dial "000" before the phone number: for international calls
  16. Q(16): Is fax transmission possible?
    A: Unfortunately, we do not handle fax transmissions.
  17. Q(17): I need to print some data. Can you assist?
    A: Only A4 size printing is available. Up to 5 pages in black and white are free at the front desk.
  18. Q(18): Is it possible to use the bath after check-out?
    A: Bath use is available until the evening of the check-out day for hotel guests only.
  19. Q(19): Can you provide assistance with taxi services during my stay?
    A: For taxi services other than airport pick-up, please consult the hotel travel desk.
    Contact Information:
    SUKURA HOLIDAYS: +91-124-4604644 (24-hour English support)
  20. Q(20): Tell me about airport transfers.
    A: Airport drop-off is available for 2,050Rs+TAX per vehicle. Please provide your flight details upon reservation.
  21. Q(21): Do you provide information on nearby golf courses and offer rental golf sets?
    A: Information on nearby golf courses and rental golf sets are available at the front desk.
    * Please note that there is a limited number of rental sets available.
